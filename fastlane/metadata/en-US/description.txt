Co-Talk is a fast and secure real-time messenger.

Stay connected with friends and colleagues anytime, anywhere. Send text, images, and videos freely, and communicate with multiple people at once through group chats.

Key Features:

Real-Time Chat
- Supports 1:1 conversations and group chats
- Messages are delivered in real time
- See unread message counts at a glance

Media Sharing
- Send photos and videos easily
- Browse shared files in the chat media gallery
- Edit images with built-in tools

Friend Management
- Add, block, and hide friends
- Set profile photos and status messages

Security
- Lock the app with biometrics (Face ID, fingerprint)
- Secure data encryption protects your conversations

Notifications
- Get push notifications for new messages instantly
- Manage notification settings per chat room

Connect with the people who matter most with Co-Talk.
